Fit matters

Who this is, and isn't, built for.

I take on a small number of engagements at a time. The work is better when fit is honest from the start.

This is a fit if you're a health tech executive who:

  • Leads an early or growth-stage health tech company, post-product-market-fit, scaling toward the next funding milestone.
  • Has Customer Success as a critical function but isn't ready to commit to a full-time CS director.
  • Operates in a regulated environment where customer trust, compliance, and clinical accuracy are non-negotiable.
  • Wants a strategic partner who has done the job, not a coach who has only studied it.
  • Treats retention and expansion as a board-level conversation, not an afterthought.

This is probably not the right fit if you:

  • Are pre-product-market-fit and still searching for repeatable customer outcomes.
  • Need a high-volume CSM to handle day-to-day account work rather than strategic leadership.
  • Want a packaged playbook delivered without a true partnership engagement.
  • Are looking for the cheapest fractional resource rather than the most relevant one.
